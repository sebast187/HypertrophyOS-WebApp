Part I: Mechanotransduction

Muscle hypertrophy is not an accident; it is an evolutionary defense mechanism. When you subject tissue to heavy loads, you trigger mechanotransduction—the process by which your body converts severe mechanical tension into chemical signals. The master switch is mTORC1.

Part II: The Dark Room (Endocrinology)

Training is merely the stimulus; actual muscle growth occurs exclusively in the dark. The ultimate factor deciding whether you lose fat or muscle during a recomp phase is nutrient partitioning, entirely dictated by your sleep architecture.

Part III: The Creatine Illusion

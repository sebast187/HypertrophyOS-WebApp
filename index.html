GYM BLUEPRINT

The Complete 6-Month Hypertrophy System

Face Pulls (Cable)

Set cable slightly above face height. Pull the rope towards your eyes, driving elbows out and back. Squeeze your rear delts and upper back. Do not use your lower back to pull.

Dead Bug

Lie on your back, arms straight up, knees bent at 90 degrees. Slowly lower opposite arm and leg to the floor while pressing your lower back hard into the mat. Return and switch.

Single-Arm DB Row

Support yourself on a bench with one hand. Keep your back flat. Pull the dumbbell up towards your hip pocket, squeezing your back muscles at the top. Lower slowly.

Glute Kickback

Attach ankle strap to low cable. Face the machine. Keep leg straight and kick back and up, squeezing the glute at the top. Do not arch your lower back.

Preacher Curl

Rest your upper arms on the angled pad. Hold dumbbells or bar. Curl the weight up towards your shoulders without letting your elbows lift off the pad. Squeeze biceps.

Arnold Press

Start with dumbbells in front of your face, palms facing you. As you press the weights overhead, rotate your wrists so your palms face forward at the top. Reverse the motion on the way down.

Bulgarian Split Squat

Place one foot behind you on a bench. Holding dumbbells by your sides, lower your hips straight down until your front thigh is parallel to the ground. Keep your chest up.

Hack Squat

Stand on the platform with back against the pad. Shoulders under pads. Unlock safety bars. Lower your body by bending knees until thighs are parallel, then drive up through heels.

Skullcrushers

Lie on a bench holding dumbbells/bar. Keep elbows pinned pointing up. Lower the weight towards your forehead by bending only the elbows, then extend back up.

Straight Arm Pulldown

Stand facing a high cable. Keep your arms completely straight (slight bend in elbow is okay) and push the bar down in an arc to your thighs. Focus entirely on squeezing your lats.

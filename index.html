No-Big-Lifts Blueprint

Your 6-Month Hypertrophy Journey

Machine Chest Press

Adjust seat so handles are at mid-chest. Press outward, squeezing the chest, and return slowly without letting the weights clang.

Lat Pulldown

Sit tall. Pull the bar down to your upper chest by driving your elbows down and back. Don't lean too far back.

Leg Press

Place feet shoulder-width apart on the sled. Lower until knees are at 90 degrees, press up driving through the whole foot.

Cable Lateral Raise

Stand next to a low cable. Pull the cable out and up to the side until your arm is parallel to the floor. Focus on the shoulders.

Bulgarian Split Squat

Rest one foot behind you on a bench. Keep your chest up and lower your hips until your front thigh is parallel to the floor.
